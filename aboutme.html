About Me

Professional

I have spent most of my summers doing research or internships at various institutions. During the summer of 2009 I worked at an ethanol refinery called Geonet in the US Virgin Islands as dehydration technician. The next summer (2010) I did research in the Weapons Material Research Division (WMRD) at an army base in Maryland. In the summer of 2011 I did research at University of South Florida under a Research Experience for Undergraduates (REU) program. gotcha

After coming to UF in the Fall of 2011 I joined a research lab in my department the following semester and have been there since. I have published a paper within that lab titled A Highly Compliant Serpentine Shaped Polyimide Interconnect for Front-End Strain Relief in Chronic Neural Implants.

Casual

I am currently a junior at the University of Florida studying Mechanincal Engineering. I enjoy playing sports such as basketball, volleyball, tennis, football, and ultimate frisbee.

I am a part of Theatre Strike Force, the premiere improv comedy and sketch troupe at the University of Florida. I have been a part of the troupe since fall 2011 and find doing improv the most exciting and fun experience one can have.
